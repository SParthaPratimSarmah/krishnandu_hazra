Partha Pratim Sarmah

Partha Pratim Sarmah joined as a JRF cum PhD student on September 15, 2025, at IIITG, working on an ANRF-funded project titled "Design of a Distributed Resource-Aware Framework to Enhance Resiliency of Multi-UAV Networks Using a Bio-Inspired Approach."
