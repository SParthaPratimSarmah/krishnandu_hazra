My Research Interests

UAV Communication and Networks

Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) have emerged as a transformative solution in modern wireless communication networks due to their high mobility, flexibility, and rapid deployment capabilities. Unlike traditional terrestrial infrastructure, UAVs can be dynamically positioned in three-dimensional space, enabling efficient on-demand connectivity in challenging environments such as disaster-affected areas, remote regions, and dense urban scenarios.

UAV-assisted communication systems are widely applied in disaster management, emergency response, rural connectivity, precision agriculture, surveillance, and smart city ecosystems. Their ability to establish line-of-sight (LoS) communication significantly improves signal quality and coverage compared to conventional ground-based systems.

Recent advancements are driven by the integration of 5G/6G networks, IoT, edge computing, and AI. Despite these advantages, challenges remain: limited battery life, interference management, spectrum allocation, secure communication, and reliable connectivity in highly dynamic environments.

Information Retrieval

Information Retrieval (IR) focuses on designing efficient techniques and systems to search, extract, and organize relevant information from large-scale and heterogeneous data sources. With the exponential growth of data in the digital era, IR has become fundamental in enabling fast, accurate, and scalable access to information.

Modern IR systems go beyond simple keyword matching by incorporating semantic understanding, context awareness, and user intent modeling. Learning-to-rank approaches and deep learning models (including transformer-based architectures) enable context-aware retrieval, cross-lingual search, and conversational search systems.

Applications span web search engines, e-commerce, question-answering systems, digital assistants, and enterprise knowledge management. Current challenges include handling ambiguous queries, ensuring fairness, maintaining privacy, and scaling for real-time retrieval over massive datasets.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables machines to learn from data, identify patterns, and make intelligent decisions. With advancements in machine learning, deep learning, and data analytics, AI is widely applied across healthcare, communication systems, finance, and smart environments.

In healthcare, AI plays a crucial role in improving diagnosis, treatment planning, and patient monitoring. By analyzing medical data such as images, signals, and patient records, AI assists in early disease detection and personalized treatment recommendations.

Recent research focuses on wearable devices for health monitoring. Modern smartwatches continuously collect physiological data — heart rate, activity levels, sleep patterns. By applying AI and ML to this data, it becomes possible to identify patterns related to health conditions including emotional and mental states.

Social Networks

Social Network Analysis (SNA) studies the structure, dynamics, and behavior of interconnected systems where entities (nodes) are linked through relationships (edges). By modeling such systems as graphs, SNA provides tools to analyze how information, influence, and behaviors propagate across networks.

Key research directions include information diffusion and influence propagation (how ideas, trends, or misinformation spread), community detection to uncover hidden group structures, and user behavior analysis. Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) enable learning complex node and edge representations, improving link prediction, node classification, and recommendation systems.

SNA has diverse applications across marketing, public opinion analysis, recommendation systems, cybersecurity, healthcare (epidemic spread modeling), and organizational behavior analysis.
